Why choose WebDock?

Blazing Fast

Bypasses bulky browser rendering chrome, delivering unmatched speed and drastically reducing your machine's RAM footprint.

Sandboxed Security

Each app runs in a fully partitioned environment. No cross-site tracking, bleeding cookies, or rogue extensions.

Unified Workspace

Combine GitHub, Figma, Spotify, Slack and more into a single elegant sidebar. Stay focused and reclaim your productivity.

Native Integration

Feels like a true desktop application with custom window controls, keyboard shortcuts, and deep OS-level integration.
